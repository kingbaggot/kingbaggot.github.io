The App uses the following data available from your device. We do not collect any personal data and only collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the App and other apps we may develop. We use this data for the legitimate interest of monitoring the App and its use.

Camera Access

The App uses your device camera to locate the Augmented Reality (AR) markers and superimpose augmented reality graphics. It also allows you to take a photograph of the AR art and your surroundings. None of this image data is stored by us or within the App. It may be stored on your device and any cloud service which you have connected to your device and will be subject to any terms applicable.

Location Access

The App uses your device’s location services to determine your position in relation to specific locations around the Central London area. None of this location data is stored by us or within the App itself.

Anonymous Reporting of Problems With A Location

If you encounter a problem with a location you can report this issue which will send an automatic message to us telling us that there is a problem with the AR marker. We will be told the location only.

You may also email us to give us feedback and this is entirely voluntary. Details of how we use this data are set out below.
